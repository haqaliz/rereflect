## Qualification Checklist (before adding to list)

- [ ] SaaS company (B2B or B2C)
- [ ] 5-50 employees
- [ ] Has a product with users (not pre-launch)
- [ ] Shows signs of growth (recent funding, hiring, launches)
- [ ] Founder/PM is reachable (email, LinkedIn, or X DM)

## Demo Call Script (15 min)

**Intro (2 min):**
- Thanks for taking the call
- Quick context: what Rereflect does in one sentence
- "I'd love to learn about how you handle feedback today"

**Discovery (5 min):**
- How many feedback items do you get per week?
- Where does it come from? (Slack, Intercom, email, support?)
- Who reads it? How often?
- What happens after someone reads it?
- What's falling through the cracks?

**Demo (5 min):**
- Upload their actual data (ask for a CSV beforehand) or use sample
- Show: sentiment scores, pain point categories, churn risk flags
- Show: AI Copilot asking a question about their data
- Show: Customer 360 health scores

**Close (3 min):**
- "Based on what you shared, here's the plan that fits: [Free/Pro]"
- Offer: 3 months free Pro for honest feedback
- Ask: "Would you be open to a case study if it works well?"
- Next step: "I'll send you a signup link. Upload your data and tell me what you think by Friday?"
