## Phase 1: First 100 Signups Strategy

### Target Customer Profile (ICP)

**Primary**: SaaS founders/PMs at early-stage startups (Seed to Series A)
- Team size: 5-50 employees
- Pain: Drowning in customer feedback from multiple sources
- Current solution: Spreadsheets, Notion, or nothing
- Budget: $29-99/month is easy approval

**Why they buy**:
- Can't afford dedicated customer research team
- Need to prioritize features based on real feedback
- Want to reduce churn by catching issues early

## Signup Acquisition Channels

### 1. Product Hunt Launch (Target: 30-50 signups)

**Prep checklist**:
- [ ] Create compelling tagline (e.g., "Turn messy feedback into product decisions")
- [ ] Record 2-min demo video showing pain point → solution
- [ ] Prepare 5-10 screenshots with annotations
- [ ] Write launch day post for social media
- [ ] Line up 10+ supporters to upvote/comment at launch
- [ ] Prepare founder responses for comments

**Best practices**:
- Launch on Tuesday/Wednesday at 12:01 AM PST
- Respond to every comment within 1 hour
- Offer extended trial (30 days) for PH users
- Post on Twitter/LinkedIn at 9 AM, 12 PM, 5 PM

**Expected outcome**: 30-50 signups, 200-500 website visits

---

### 2. Founder Communities (Target: 20-30 signups)

**Where to post** (no spam, add value first):

| Community | Strategy | Link |
|-----------|----------|------|
| Indie Hackers | Share building journey, offer beta access | indiehackers.com |
| r/SaaS | Answer questions, soft mention in comments | reddit.com/r/SaaS |
| r/startups | Monthly feedback thread participation | reddit.com/r/startups |
| Twitter/X | Build in public, share insights | #buildinpublic |
| LinkedIn | Post about customer feedback challenges | Your network |

**Content ideas**:
- "How we analyzed 1,000 customer feedbacks and found 3 patterns"
- "The spreadsheet I used before building Rereflect"
- "5 signs your customers are about to churn (from feedback data)"

**Rules**:
- 80% value, 20% promotion
- Never cold pitch in DMs
- Be genuinely helpful first

---

### 3. Cold Outreach to ICP (Target: 20-30 signups)

**Finding prospects**:
- Search LinkedIn: "Head of Product" + "Series A" + your region
- Use ProductHunt shipped products (they have feedback to analyze)
- Check AngelList for funded startups

**Email template** (personalized):

```
Subject: Quick question about [Company] feedback

Hi [Name],

I noticed [Company] just launched [feature/product] on ProductHunt - congrats on the traction!

Quick question: how are you currently organizing customer feedback?

I ask because I'm building Rereflect - it uses AI to automatically categorize feedback into pain points, feature requests, and churn risks.

Would you be open to a 15-min call to see if it could help? Happy to give you 3 months free if you share honest feedback.

Cheers,
[Your name]
```

**Metrics to track**:
- Open rate target: >40%
- Reply rate target: >10%
- Demo booked target: >5%

## Pricing Strategy for Early Traction

### Early Adopter Offers

| Offer | Use Case |
|-------|----------|
| Extended trial (30 days) | Product Hunt, cold outreach |
| Lifetime deal ($299 one-time) | AppSumo-style communities |
| 3 months free for feedback | Beta testers |
| 50% off first year | Referrals |

### When to discount vs not:
- ✅ Discount for genuine early adopters who'll give feedback
- ✅ Discount for case study permission
- ❌ Don't discount for price objection (wrong ICP)
- ❌ Don't discount publicly (devalues product)
